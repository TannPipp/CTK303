Later in 2003, after being discovered by Eminem and sign onto Shady Records, he released his first major-label album, "Get Rich or Die Tryin" which also the same album that "In da Club".

About the Song: The Song was released on January 7, 2003 and was considered to be the lead single of the album. shorterly after peaked #1 on the US billboard top 100 (Fun Fact: It was the top song in the US the week of my birthday).
